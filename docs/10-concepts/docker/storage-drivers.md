## Storage Drivers
The storage driver is in charge of the storage and management of images and containers on the host, we have a few options here: 
- containerd
- overlay2
- fuse-overlayfs
- btrfs
- zfs
- vfs

## overlay2
Backing filesystem: Most common filesystems (e.g., xfs, ext4, btrfs)

## fuse-overlayfs
Backing filesystem: any

## btrfs
Backing filesystem: btrfs

## zfs
Backing filesystem: zfs

## vfs
Backing filesystem: any
